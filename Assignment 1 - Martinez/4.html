66th Room

You did everything you could ( to no avail there was no response ) and you can feel a presence behind you until...a cold hand covers your mouth and tells you to keep quiet. You turned around and be shocked until you take a closer look only to see a very malnourished pale student...since you remember that you study medicine... you see that she must have been affected by the Marie Antoinette Syndrome...and you can see visible choke bruises on her neck.

By: Gail Martinez
